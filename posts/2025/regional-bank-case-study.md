A regional bank with branch operations across Cambodia holds an
average of **240 internal meetings a week**. Before adopting SMEAN,
most of those meetings either went undocumented or required dedicated
minute-takers — a costly bottleneck that quietly degraded handoffs
between branches.

> *Names and exact figures have been anonymized at the customer's
> request. The patterns are representative of multi-branch
> financial-services deployments we've worked with.*

## The numbers

After a six-month rollout, the operations team estimates:

- **110 hours per month** saved in manual documentation
- **4× increase** in meetings that get any kind of written record
- **30% reduction** in cross-branch information loss during handoffs

## What changed

Audit, compliance, and onboarding workflows have all benefited. New
hires now have a searchable archive of policy meetings going back a
year; auditors no longer wait for minute-takers to circulate notes.

The deployment integrates with the bank's internal SSO and routes
each meeting summary to the relevant ledger in their document
management system — no manual filing required.
